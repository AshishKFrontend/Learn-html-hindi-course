üìß Chapter 14: HTML Email Links

Jab aap chahte hain ki link click karte hi user ka Email App (Outlook/Gmail) khul jaye aur aapka email address pehle se likha ho, toh hum mailto: ka use karte hain.

1. Basic Mailto

Isse bas ek khali email compose window khulti hai jisme "To" field mein aapka email hota hai.

<a href="mailto:ashish@example.com">Humein Email Karein</a>

2. Advanced Mailto (Subject & Body)

Aap pehle se hi Subject aur Message bhi likh kar bhej sakte hain taaki user ko type na karna pade.

Syntax Logic:

  • ?subject=... : Subject line ke liye.
  • &body=... : Message body ke liye (Doosri cheez ke liye & lagta hai).
<a href="mailto:support@ashish.com?subject=Help Needed&body=Hello Sir, I have a doubt..."> Support Se Baat Karein </a>
