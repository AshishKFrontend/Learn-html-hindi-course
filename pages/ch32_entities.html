üî° Chapter 32: HTML Entities (Special Symbols)

HTML Entities ka use aise characters dikhane ke liye hota hai jo keyboard par nahi hote ya HTML code mein "Reserved" hote hain. Har entity & (Ampersand) se shuru hoti hai aur ; (Semicolon) par khatam hoti hai.

1. Reserved Characters

Kyunki < aur >brauzar dvaara taig samajhane ke lie yooz hote hain, isalie unhen tekst kee tarah dikhaane ke lie hamen in kods kee zaroorat padatee hai:

<!-- Screen par

Hello

dikhane ke liye --> &lt;h1&gt;Hello&lt;/h1&gt;

2. The Magic Space: &nbsp; (Non-breaking Space)

HTML mein agar aap 10 spaces denge, toh browser sirf 1 hi dikhayega. Zyada spaces dene ke liye &nbsp; ka use hota hai.

Extra Tip: Ye do shabdon ko alag lines par "tootne" se bhi rokta hai (e.g., 10&nbsp;km).

3. Copyright & Commercial Symbols

Ye symbols footer aur business websites ke liye zaroori hain:

  • © Copyright: &copy;
  • ™ Trademark: &trade;
  • ® Registered: &reg;
  • ₹ Indian Rupee: &#8377;
