HTML file save karte waqt aur browser ko batate waqt humein Encoding ka dhyan rakhna padta hai taaki symbols aur languages sahi se dikhen.

1. ASCII (American Standard)

Ye 1960s ka sabse purana standard hai. Isme sirf 128 characters hote hain.

  • Range: 0-127
  • Covers: English (A-Z, a-z), Numbers (0-9), basic symbols (@, #).
  • Kami: Isme Hindi ya Emojis ke liye koi jagah nahi hai.
