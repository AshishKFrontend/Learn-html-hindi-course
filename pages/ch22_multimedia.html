HTML5 ke aane se pehle video/audio ke liye extra plugins (jaise Flash Player) chahiye hote the, par ab <video> aur <audio> tags sabse powerful hain.

1. Video Tag: Har Attribute ka Post-mortem

Video dikhane ke liye sirf tag likhna kaafi nahi hai, uske attributes use control karte hain:

  • src: Video file ka path (URL).
  • controls: Iske bina user video play nahi kar payega (Play, Volume, Fullscreen buttons gayab rahenge).
  • muted: Video ki awaaz band rakhta hai.
  • poster: Jab tak video load ho rahi hai, ek image (thumbnail) dikhane ke liye.
  • loop: Video khatam hote hi dobara shuru ho jayegi.

‚ö†Ô∏è Autoplay Policy: Google Chrome aur modern browsers ki policy hai ki autoplay tabhi kaam karega jab video muted ho. (Bina awaaz ke hi auto-play allowed hai).

<video src="movie.mp4" controls width="400" poster="thumb.jpg"> Aapka browser video support nahi karta. </video>

2. Audio Tag: Deep Control

Audio tag bhi lagbhag video jaisa hi hai par isme koi screen nahi hoti.

  • controls: Player interface dikhane ke liye zaroori hai.
  • autoplay / muted / loop: Same as video.
<audio src="song.mp3" controls loop> Audio file support nahi hai. </audio>
