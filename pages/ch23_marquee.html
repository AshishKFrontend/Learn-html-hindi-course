Is chapter mein hum external files ko mount karna aur text animations (scrolling) ke baare mein seekhenge.

1. Embed vs Object

<embed>

Ye ek simple tag hai jo PDF, Flash ya external plugins ko turant "mount" kar deta hai.

<object>

Ye "Smart" tag hai. Agar specified file nahi chalti, toh ye Fallback Content dikha deta hai.

<object data="notes.pdf" type="application/pdf" width="300" height="200"> <p>Aapka browser PDF nahi dikha sakta. <a href="notes.pdf">Download karein</a>.</p> </object>
