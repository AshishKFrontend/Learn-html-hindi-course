HTML document shuru karte waqt hum <html> tag mein lang attribute ka use karte hain taaki browser ko page ki bhasha pata chal sake.

1. lang Attribute (Kyun Zaroori Hai?)

Google Translate

Agar aapne lang="hi" likha hai, toh foreign users ko Chrome apne aap "Translate to English?" ka option dega.

Screen Readers

Sahi bhasha batane se visually impaired log software ke sahi accent (uchharan) mein content sun paate hain.

<!-- Hindi Website ke liye --> <html lang="hi">
