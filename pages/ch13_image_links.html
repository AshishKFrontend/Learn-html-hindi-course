Links sirf text par hi nahi, images par bhi lagaye ja sakte hain. Is chapter mein hum Clickable Images banana seekhenge.

1. Image as a Link

Iske liye bas <img> tag ko <a> tag ke andar band kar dete hain.

<a href="https://google.com"> <img src="logo.png" alt="Google Logo"> </a>

2. Image Maps (Advanced)

Image map ka matlab hai ek hi photo par alag-alag jagah click karne par alag-alag links khulna. (Jaise India ke map par UP par click karein to UP ki info khule, aur MP par MP ki).

Zaroori Tags & Attributes:

  • usemap="#name": Image tag ke andar lagta hai (Hash # zaroori hai).
  • <map name="name">: Map define karne ke liye.
  • <area>: Clickable area banane ke liye.

Shapes & Coords (Aakar aur Jagah):

Shape Description Coords (Example)
rect Rectangle (Chaukor) x1, y1, x2, y2 (Top-Left, Bottom-Right)
circle Circle (Gola) x, y, radius (Center, Radius)
poly Polygon (Teda-Meda) x1,y1, x2,y2, x3,y3...
